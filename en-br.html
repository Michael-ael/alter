How we protect your money

A thorough verification process
Every user verifies their email, occupation, and identity with a valid government ID. This KYC (Know Your Customer) process protects you from fraud and identity theft.
Two-factor authentication
2FA adds an extra layer of security to your account, ensuring secure access and protecting your transactions with new or saved beneficiaries.
Devices and sessions management
This feature lets you see all devices logged into your Grey account across web and mobile apps. You can remove suspicious devices to keep your account secure from unauthorized access.
