Best digital nomad rentals: affordable living options in Montserrat

Adeolu Titus Adekunle

SHARE THIS POST

Share on Twitter

Montserrat, called the Emerald Isle of the Caribbean, is a hidden gem in the Caribbean that deserves more attention. Its breathtaking landscapes, beautiful beaches, and quiet lifestyle create the right environment for creativity and productivity. As an emerging destination for digital nomads, Montserrat offers the serenity of nature and a unique cultural experience, all with affordable housing options.

Finding a place to stay is often a challenge for digital nomads moving to a new city or country. This guide explores the best digital nomad rentals and affordable living options in Montserrat to help make your move easier.

Read also: The most miraculous digital nomad cities in the world in 2024

Why should digital nomads choose Montserrat?

While not as popular as some digital nomad hotspots, Montserrat offers various benefits that make it a paradise for digital nomads.

Affordable lifestyle and welcoming people: Montserrat has a small population, which creates  a calm and peaceful setting. Compared to other Caribbean islands, the cost of living in Montserrat is affordable, with budget-friendly accommodation options, fresh food and activities.

Reliable connectivity: Montserrat has a solid internet infrastructure, making it easy for digital nomads to stay connected. Many accommodations and coworking-friendly cafes provide high-speed internet, ensuring the perfect remote work experience.

Also read: 5 simple ways for digital nomads to always stay connected

Nature and activities: From the watching the Soufrière Hills volcano to the stretch of beaches like Woodlands and Rendezvous Bay, Montserrat is a place for nature lovers and explorers. Popular activities include hiking, snorkelling, and bird-watching.

Visa options for digital nomads: Montserrat welcomes remote workers through its Montserrat Remote Work Stamp. It is a digital nomad visa designed to attract foreigners looking to live and work on the island. The stamp is valid for up to 12 months, with the possibility of renewal.

Best digital nomad rentals: affordable living options in Montserrat

There are various affordable housing options in Montserrat, including:

Guesthouses and budget-friendly apartments

There are many guesthouses and budget-friendly housing options in Montserrat with monthly rental rates. Most of these accommodations have a kitchen, Wi-Fi, and are conveniently located near local tourist attractions, restaurants and mini-marts. Here are some great options for you:

  • Jasmine Suites: If you are looking for a laidback, serene community, then the Runaway Pointe Circle is one of the best options. Jasmine Suites offers a  one-bedroom apartment with an ensuite bathroom, fully furnished kitchen, access to a swimming pool and a mature garden.
  • Manjack Hill-View Retreat: This is a great two-bedroom apartment option in Manjack if you'd need more space for   your friends and family. The apartments are fully furnished and has laundry and Wi-Fi facilities, and prices start from as low as $100.
  • Maczel Apartments: Situated in Judy Piece and close to the town centre, Maczel Apartments offer five- and six-bedroom apartments with stunning ocean and mountain views, making it a great option for views best suited if you are traveling in a group.
  • Chez Mango Villas: Located in Woodlands, these villas come with  modern facilities and high-speed internet, perfect for remote work.
  • Cudjoe Head Rentals: For minimalists, this quieter part of the island offers affordable one-bedroom apartments and shared accommodations.

Long-term vacation rentals

Many vacation homes in Montserrat offer discounts for long-term stays:

  • Villa rentals in Old Towne and Olveston: These areas are popular among expatriates and nomads, offering affordable rental villas with private workspaces.
  • Airbnb: Platforms like Airbnb have a variety of options, from private rooms to entire homes, with discounts for stays exceeding 28 days.

Shared housing options

Connect with local digital nomad communities to get information about shared housing. The Montserrat nomad network online often post housing opportunities, and can help you find roommates to split costs.

Cottages

You can opt for a cottage in Silver Hills. This region is budget-friendly and offers a peaceful environment with excellent hiking trails and opportunities to explore nature.

Conclusion

Montserrat is an interesting destination for digital nomads seeking a peaceful, inspiring environment to work and live. Its stunning natural beauty, welcoming community, and robust internet make it an ideal place to work and have fun. With the Montserrat Remote Work Stamp, relocating to this Caribbean paradise is now easier than ever.Whether you're looking to escape the hustle and bustle of cities or explore nature while staying productive, Montserrat promises a digital nomad experience like no other.
