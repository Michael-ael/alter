Frequently asked questions

Everything You Need to Know About Using Grey Business

How can I open a Grey Business account?

It’s pretty easy. Simply fill out a short form about your business. We’ll review it and learn more about what you do, along with your needs. After this, we will onboard you, and your business account will be ready for use.

How long will it take to get a Grey Business account?

The form only takes a few minutes to fill out. We will typically review your application in a few business days and give feedback within a week. However, this might be subject to how complex your business is and the nature of the documents submitted. If you have any questions concerning your application, please contact our support team.
