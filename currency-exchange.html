How fast will I receive my converted funds?

Once your conversion is complete, you can withdraw your funds to your bank account instantly, or within a few business hours, depending on the currency.

What currencies can I convert with Grey?

Grey supports over 40 currencies, including USD, EUR, GBP, etc. You can convert any major currency in seconds.

Are there any fees for converting currencies?

Grey offers transparent pricing with no hidden fees. The rate you see is the rate you get.

Is Grey secure?

Yes, we use bank-grade encryption and are regulated by FinCEN and FINTRAC for maximum security.

Can I use Grey in multiple countries?

Absolutely! Grey is designed for global use, perfect for digital nomads and travelers.

Can I receive payments from international clients directly?

Yes, you can receive payments in multiple currencies and convert them instantly.
